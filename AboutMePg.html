About Me

Hi, my name is Rishabh Laheja. I am a passionate animator and a comic book writer. Ever since I was a child, animated TV shows and movies kept me hooked to the TV screen with questions in my mind such as - Who came up with this idea of this series/movie and how did they make it? I drew my first comic book when I was 13 years old, about 200 pages long of the story I always saw in my dreams and the glimpses of what I created in my diary. From here my journey and passion for comic books and animation began. I was first introduced to the world of CG when I started using Blender and then Maya. I have famaliarized myself with various technologies concerning CG - Maya, Blender, Unity and Unreal Engine each of which mesmerizes me with their ability to make CG a reality. As part of my Bachelor's (in Computer Science) project I created a 3D Third Person fighting game in Unreal Engine 4. But in each of these events the only thing I loved the most was to watch a character interact with the environment and how to make the character feel real. My love for animation burgeoned when I began learning the basics of animation under the mentorship of James Lyon. Today after a course of 7 months into animation with my mentors being James Lyons and Kevin Jackson of Animsquad I venture the world of animation with immense passion and a desire to continue this path of giving life to characters by the "Power Of Animation!!!"
